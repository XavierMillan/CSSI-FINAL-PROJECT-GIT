Why we created Student-Oriented Coding

Computer science education in public schools still remains widely inaccessible, especially to underrepresented groups. Research shows that learning computer programming at a young age boosts problem solving and computational thinking skills. But only 26% of elementary schools in the nation offer these classes.1

Mission

To encourage elementary school students to learn about computer science.

Vision

An accessible and inclusive computer science education for everyone.
